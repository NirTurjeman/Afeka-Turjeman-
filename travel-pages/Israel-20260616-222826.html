Travel Tips

  • Carry a reusable water bottle – the desert can be harsh.
  • Respect local customs, especially during religious holidays.
  • Use public transport or bike-sharing in major cities.
  • Try local street food – it’s a culinary adventure.
  • Plan your visit to historical sites early in the morning to avoid crowds.
