Top Attractions

Explore the ancient streets of Jerusalem's Old City, a UNESCO World Heritage site rich with history.

Enjoy the vibrant beaches and nightlife of Tel Aviv, Israel’s cosmopolitan hub.

Visit the historic fortress of Masada, perched atop a desert plateau with breathtaking views.

Float in the buoyant waters of the Dead Sea, the lowest point on Earth.

Experience communal living and agricultural innovation at a Kibbutz.

Travel Tips

Language

Hebrew and Arabic are official; English is widely spoken.

Currency

Israeli New Shekel (ILS). Credit cards accepted everywhere.

Best Time to Visit

Spring (March–May) and Fall (September–November) offer mild weather.

Transport

Public buses, trains, and shared taxis (sheruts) are reliable.

Safety

Stay aware of local news; avoid restricted areas during tensions.
