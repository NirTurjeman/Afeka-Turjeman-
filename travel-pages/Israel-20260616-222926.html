Top Attractions

Masada

Ancient fortress overlooking the Dead Sea, rich in history and stunning views.

Dead Sea

Float in the world's saltiest lake and enjoy mineral-rich mud treatments.

Tel Aviv

Vibrant city known for its beaches, nightlife, and modern architecture.

Jerusalem Old City

Explore the holy sites, markets, and centuries-old streets.

Haifa

Stunning botanical gardens and a mix of cultures along the Mediterranean.

Travel Tips

  • Carry a reusable water bottle; tap water is safe in most areas.
  • Respect local customs—dress modestly when visiting religious sites.
  • Use public transport or ride‑share apps for convenient travel.
  • Plan for sun protection; the sun can be intense, especially in summer.
  • Try local street food—falafel, shawarma
