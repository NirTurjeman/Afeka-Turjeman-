Travel Tips

  • Carry a reusable water bottle – tap water is safe to drink.
  • Respect local customs, especially during religious holidays.
  • Use public transportation or ride‑hailing apps for city travel.
  • Pack light clothing for daytime and a jacket for cooler evenings.
  • Stay hydrated and use sunscreen in the desert regions.
